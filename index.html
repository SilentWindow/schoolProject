About us


We are (ABC Corporation?). We formed this company in order to provide onboarding services to startup IT companies on industry standards and best practices of IT Ethics and Security. We feel that we can best serve our clients by providing compact and easy to read ethical and security best practices. With our services, our clients and their employees will have 24/7 access to this information, greatly reducing time and cost to training employees.
